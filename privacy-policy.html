Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

IAALearn ("we," "our," or "us") is developed and operated by Mighty Sky Technologies Ltd. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by IAALearn.

By using the IAALearn mobile application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, name (when you register)
  • Authentication Data: OAuth tokens from Google/GitHub login
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store card details)

2.2 Usage Data

  • App Interactions: Pages visited, features used, session duration
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics collects anonymized usage statistics

2.3 Interview Data

  • Session Content: Questions asked and answers generated during practice sessions
  • Audio Transcriptions: Voice input during interview practice (processed for transcription only)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining the IAALearn service
  • Authenticating your account and processing payments
  • Improving and personalizing your experience
  • Communicating with you about updates and support
  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve app functionality
  • Detecting and preventing fraud and abuse

4. Third-Party Services

IAALearn uses the following third-party services:

4.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Analytics, authentication, cloud storage
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.2 Stripe

  • Purpose: Payment processing for subscriptions
  • Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

4.3 Supabase

  • Purpose: Database and authentication
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

4.4 AI Providers

  • Purpose: Generating interview answers and transcriptions
  • Note: Audio input is processed by AI services; no audio is stored on our servers

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel
  • Data is stored on secure servers with regular security audits

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active; you may request deletion
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized data retained for up to 14 months
  • Interview Sessions: Retained for 30 days unless you request earlier deletion

7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("Right to be forgotten")
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: Opt out of non-essential data collection

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@mightyskytech.com

8. Children's Privacy

IAALearn is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
